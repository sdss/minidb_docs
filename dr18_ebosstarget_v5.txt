Summary
-------

 A catalog of targets, selected from SDSS+WISE imaging that were considered for observation in the 
 SDSS-IV/eBOSS project, including spectrophotometric starts and candidate QSOs.
 This catalogue is a data product of the ebosstarget target selection software, version "v5_0".
