Summary
-------
Catalog from Kevin Schlaufman for the selection of bright metal-poor stars from the method of Schlaufman and Casey (2014): https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2014ApJ...797...13S/abstract. WISE values from WHICH CATALOG, 2MASS values from 2MASS point source catalog.


Columns
-------

designation - 2MASS Designation []
ra_1 - right ascension from the AllWISE catalog [degrees]
dec_1 - declination from the AllWISE catalog [degrees]
glon - Galactic longitude from AllWISE catalog [degrees]
glat - Galactic latitude from the AllWISE catalog [degrees]
w1mpro - AllWISE W1-band magnitude measured with profile-fitting photometry [mag] 
w2mpro - AllWISE W2-band magnitude measured with profile-fitting photometry [mag]
w3mpro - AllWISE W3-band magnitude measured with profile-fitting photometry [mag]
w4mpro - AllWISE W4-band magnitude measured with profile-fitting photometry [mag]
pmra - Gaia DR2 proper motion [mas/yr]

j_m_2mass - 2MASS J=band magnitude [mag]

k_m_2mass - 2MASS magnitude [mag]
ra_2 -  Right ascension from the 2MASS catalog [degrees]
raerr - Uncertainty in right ascension from the 2MASS catalog [degrees]
dec_2 - Declination from the 2MASS catalog [degrees]
decerr - Uncertainty in declination from the 2MASS catalog [degrees]
