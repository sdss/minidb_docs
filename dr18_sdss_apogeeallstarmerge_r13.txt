Summary
-------

List of APOGEE DR16 stars for RV followup


Columns
-------

apogee_id - 2MASS style ID from APOGEE DR16 []
nvisits - Number of visits into combined spectra, accross all allStar entries for the star []
nentries - Number of unique allStar entries for the star []
ra - Right ascension (J2000) [degrees]
dec - Declination (J2000) [degrees]
glon - Galactic longitude [degrees]
glat - Galactic latitude [degrees]
pmra - proper motion in RA [mas/yr]
pmdec - proper motion in DEC [mas/yr]
pm_src - source of proper motion (e.g. gaia) []
j - 2MASS J mag [bad=99] [mag]
j_err - Uncertainty in 2MASS J mag [mag]
h - 2MASS H mag [bad=99] [mag]
h_err - Uncertainty in 2MASS H mag [mag] 
k - 2MASS Ks mag [bad=99] [mag]
k_err - Uncertainty in 2MASS Ks mag [mag]
ak - K-band extinction adopted for targetting [mag]
vhelio_avg - Average radial velocity, weighted by S/N, rederived to reflect all entries [km/s]
vhelio_err - Uncertainty in VHELIO_AVG from the S/N-weighted individual RVs, rederived to reflect all entries [km/s]
vscatter - Scatter of individual visit RVs around average, rederived to reflect all entries [km/s]
sig_rvvar - Measure of the significance of the star's RV variability (see Troup, et. al 2016) []
baseline - Temporal baseline of the observations (JD_last-JD-first) [days]
mean_fiber - The mean fiberID of all of the star's visits []
sig_fiber - The standard deviation of the fiberID of all of the star's visits []
apstar_ids - List of APSTAR_ID from each of the star's allStar entries []
visits - List of VISIT_ID from eeach of the star's allVisit entries []
fields - List of FIELD flags from each of the star's allStar entries []
surveys - List of SURVEY flags from each of the star's allStar entries []
telescopes - List of TELESCOPE flags from each of the star's allStar entries []
targflags - Verbose/text form of TARGFLAG combined from all entries []
starflags - Verbose/text form of STARFLAG combined from all entries []
aspcapflags - Verbose/text form of ASPCAPFLAG combined from all entries []
teff - Teff from ASPCAP analysis of combined spectra (from PARAM) averaged across entries [K]
teff_err - Teff uncertainty (from PARAM_COV) [K]
logg - log g from ASPCAP analysis of combined spectrum (from PARAM) averaged across entries [dex]
logg_err - log g uncertainty (from PARAM_COV) [dex]
feh - metallicity from ASPCAP analysis of combined spectrum (from PARAM) averaged across entries [dex]
feh_err - feh uncertainty (from PARAM_COV) [dex]
startype - Best guess of star's evolutionary state based on stellar parameters or external catalogs (RC=red clump, RG=red giant, SG = subgiant, MS=dwarf, PMS=pre-main sequence) []
vjitter - Atmospheric RV jitter derived from log g using the relationship derived in Hekker, et. al 2008. [km/s]  
dist - Derived or given distance to the star [pc]
dist_err - uncertainy of the distance [pc]
dist_src - Source of the star's distance measurement (e.g. gaia=derived from gaia parallax) []
mstar - Derived or given stellar mass (Solar Mass)
mstar_err - Uncertainty of the Derived stellar mass [Solar Mass]
rstar - Derived stellar radius [Solar Radius]
rstar_err - Uncertainty of the Derived stellar radius [Solar Radius]
mstar_src - Source of the star's Mass (if not derived) []
designation - identifier []
