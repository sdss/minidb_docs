Summary
-------
Pre-main sequence (PMS) catalogue from Zari+2018.



Columns
-------

source - Unique source identifier (Gaia DR2 source_id) []
glon - Galactic longitude [deg]
glat - Galactic latitude [deg]
plx - Parallax [mas]
e_plx - Standard error of parallax (parallax_error) [mas]
pmglon - Proper motion in galactic longitude [mas/yr]
e_pmglon - Standard error of proper motion in galactic longitude [mas/yr]
pmglat - Proper motion in galactic latitude [mas/yr]
e_pmglat - Standard error of proper motion in galactic latitude [mas/yr]
pmlbcorr - Correlation between proper motion in galactic longitude and proper motion in galactic latitude []
rv - radial velocity [km/s]
e_rv - radial velocity error [km/s]
gmag - G-band mean magnitude (phot_g_mean_mag) [mag]
bpmag - BP-band mean magnitude (phot_bp_mean_mag) [mag]
rpmag - RP-band mean magnitude (phot_g_mean_mag) [mag]
bp_over_rp - BP/RP excess factor []
chi2al - AL chi-square value (astrometric_chi2_al) []
ngal - Number of good observation AL (astrometric_n_good_obs_al) []
ag - Extinction in G-band (A_G) [mag]
bp_rp - Colour excess in BP-RP [mag]
uwe - Unit Weight Error, as defined in Lindegren et al., 2018A&A...616A...2L []
