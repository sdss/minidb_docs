Summary
-------

This table contains non-single-star orbital models for sources compatible with an orbital two-body solution, copied from the Gaia DR3 archive. Form more details refer to https://gea.esac.esa.int/archive/documentation/GDR3/Gaia_archive/chap_datamodel/sec_dm_non--single_stars_tables/ssec_dm_nss_two_body_orbit.html.


Columns
-------

solution_id - Solution Identifier
source_id - Source Identifier. Matches the source_id column in the dr20_gaia_dr3_source table.
nss_solution_type - NSS model adopted
ra - Right ascension [deg]
ra_error - Standard error of right ascension [mas]
dec - Declination [deg]
dec_error - Standard error of declination [mas]
parallax - Parallax [mas]
parallax_error - Standard error of parallax [mas]
pmra - Proper motion in right ascension direction [mas yr−1]
pmra_error - Standard error of proper motion in right ascension direction [mas yr−1]
pmdec - Proper motion in declination direction [mas yr−1]
pmdec_error - Standard error of proper motion in declination direction [mas yr−1]
a_thiele_innes - Thiele-Innes element A [mas]
a_thiele_innes_error - Standard error of Thiele-Innes element A [mas]
b_thiele_innes - Thiele-Innes element B [mas]
b_thiele_innes_error - Standard error of Thiele-Innes element B [mas]
f_thiele_innes - Thiele-Innes element F [mas]
f_thiele_innes_error - Standard error of Thiele-Innes element F [mas]
g_thiele_innes - Thiele-Innes element G [mas]
g_thiele_innes_error - Standard error of Thiele-Innes element G [mas]
c_thiele_innes - C element of Thiele-Innes [AU]
c_thiele_innes_error - Standard error of C element of Thiele-Innes [AU]
h_thiele_innes - H element of Thiele-Innes [AU]
h_thiele_innes_error - Standard error of H element of Thiele-Innes [AU]
period - Orbital Period [day]
period_error - Standard error of Orbital Period [day]
t_periastron - Periastron epoch [day]
t_periastron_error - Standard error of Periastron epoch [day]
eccentricity - eccentricity
eccentricity_error - Standard error of eccentricity
center_of_mass_velocity - The velocity of the centre of mass [km s−1]
center_of_mass_velocity_error - Standard error of The velocity of the centre of mass [km s−1]
semi_amplitude_primary - Semi-amplitude of the centre of mass [km s−1]
semi_amplitude_primary_error - Standard error of Semi-amplitude of the centre of mass [km s−1]
semi_amplitude_secondary - The semiamplitude of the radial velocity curve for second component [km s−1]
semi_amplitude_secondary_error - Standard error of The semiamplitude of the radial velocity curve for second component [km s−1]
mass_ratio - Mass ratio
mass_ratio_error - Standard error of Mass ratio
fill_factor_primary - Fill factor of primary
fill_factor_primary_error - Standard error of Fill factor of primary
fill_factor_secondary - Fill factor of secondary
fill_factor_secondary_error - Standard error of Fill factor of secondary
inclination - Orbital inclination [deg]
inclination_error - Standard error of Orbital inclination [deg]
arg_periastron - Argument of periastron [deg]
arg_periastron_error - Standard error of Argument of periastron [deg]
temperature_ratio - Ratio of the effective temperatures
temperature_ratio_error - Standard error of the ratio of the effective temperatures
temperature_ratio_definition - Code defining which fitting scenario did apply to the effective temperature
astrometric_n_obs_al - Total astrometric CCD observations in AL considered
astrometric_n_good_obs_al - Total astrometric CCD observations in AL actually used
rv_n_obs_primary - Total number of radial velocities considered for the primary
rv_n_good_obs_primary - Total number of radial velocities actually used for the primary
rv_n_obs_secondary - Total number of radial velocities considered for the secondary in the case of SB2
rv_n_good_obs_secondary - Total number of radial velocities actually used for the secondary in the case of SB2
phot_g_n_obs - Total number of G photometry measurements considered
phot_g_n_good_obs - Total number of G photometry measurements actually used
bit_index - boolean mask for the fields above in the corr_vec matrix
corr_vec - Vector form of the upper triangle of the correlation matrix (column-major ordered)
obj_func - value of the objective function at the solution
goodness_of_fit - goodness of fit in the Hipparcos sense
efficiency - Efficiency of the solution
significance - The significance of the solution (i.e. how worth keeping a model is)
flags - Quality flag for the achieved NSS solution
conf_spectro_period - The probability of the period for not being due to (gaussian white) noise. Relevant for SB1, SB1C, SB2 and SB2C models. To be ignored otherwise.
r_pole_sum - Sum of the polar radii of primary and secondary (in units of the semi-major axis)
r_l1_point_sum - L1-pointing radii of primary and secondary (in units of the semi-major axis)
r_spher_sum - Sum of the radii of sphere having the same volume as the primary and secondary (in units of the semi-major axis)
ecl_time_primary - Time of mid-eclipse of the primary by the secondary [Julian Date (day)]
ecl_time_secondary - Time of mid-eclipse of the secondary by the primary [Julian Date (day)]
ecl_dur_primary - Duration of primary eclipse assuming spherical components [day]
ecl_dur_secondary - Duration of secondary eclipse assuming spherical components [day]
g_luminosity_ratio - Ratio of the G-band luminosity of the secondary over the primary
input_period_error - Standard error of the period taken from vari_eclipsing_binary.frequency_error [day]
g_rank - Rank of the G-band solution
astrometric_jitter - Uncorrelated astrometric jitter term [mas]
