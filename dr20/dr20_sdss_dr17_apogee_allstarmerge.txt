Summary
-------

In the SDSS-IV APOGEE-2, if a star was observed in multiple fields or multiple survey modes (e.g., observed at APO vs. LCO), then the star would end up with multiple entries in the summary allStar file. This table merges all multiple entries for stars in the DR17 APOGEE allStar file. This file was primarily used for target selection for the mwm_bin_rv_long carton.


Columns
-------

apogee_id - 2MASS style ID from APOGEE DR16 []
nvisits - Number of visits into combined spectra, accross all allStar entries for the star []
nentries - Number of unique allStar entries for the star []
ra - Right ascension (J2000) [degrees]
dec - Declination (J2000) [degrees]
glon - Galactic longitude [degrees]
glat - Galactic latitude [degrees]
pmra - proper motion in RA [mas/yr]
pmdec - proper motion in DEC [mas/yr]
pm_src - source of proper motion (e.g. gaia) []
j - 2MASS J mag [bad=99] [mag]
j_err - Uncertainty in 2MASS J mag [mag]
h - 2MASS H mag [bad=99] [mag]
h_err - Uncertainty in 2MASS H mag [mag]
k - 2MASS Ks mag [bad=99] [mag]
k_err - Uncertainty in 2MASS Ks mag [mag]
ak - K-band extinction adopted for targetting [mag]
vhelio_avg - Average radial velocity, weighted by S/N, rederived to reflect all entries [km/s]
vhelio_err - Uncertainty in VHELIO_AVG from the S/N-weighted individual RVs, rederived to reflect all entries [km/s]
vscatter - Scatter of individual visit RVs around average, rederived to reflect all entries [km/s]
sig_rvvar - Measure of the significance of the star's RV variability (see Troup, et. al 2016) []
baseline - Temporal baseline of the observations (JD_last-JD-first) [days]
mean_fiber - The mean fiberID of all of the star's visits []
sig_fiber - The standard deviation of the fiberID of all of the star's visits []
stars_pk - Indices of the star’s allStar entries (from DR17)
nvisits_entry - Number of visits associated with each of the star’s DR17 allStar entries
snr_entry - Combined spectrum S/N for each of the star’s DR17 allStar entries
telescopes - List of TELESCOPE flags from each of the star's DR17 allStar entries
fields - Field associated with each of the star’s DR17 allStar entries
targflags - Text form of APOGEE target flags concatenated from the star’s DR17 allStar entries
starflags -  Text form of APOGEE reduction flags concatenated from the star’s DR17 allStar entries
aspcapflags -  Text form of ASPCAP analysis flags concatenated from the star’s DR17 allStar entries
member - Text form of the APOGEE cluster membership flag  concatenated from the star’s DR17 allStar entries
gaia_source_id - Associated Gaia DR3 source ID []
dist - Derived or given distance to the star [pc]
dist_err - Uncertainty of the distance [pc]
dist_src - Source of the star's distance measurement (e.g. gaia=derived from gaia parallax) []
teff_avg - SNR-weighted average of the star’s allStar T_eff entries from DR17
teff_avg_err - Uncertainty in teff_avg
logg_avg -  SNR-weighted average of the star’s allStar log g entries from DR17
logg_avg_err -  Uncertainty in logg_avg
m_h_avg -  SNR-weighted average of the star’s allStar [M/H] entries from DR17
m_h_avg_err -  Uncertainty in m_h_avg
visits_pk - The indices of the star’s DR17 allVisit entries included in visit combination
