Summary
-------

Catalog of pre-main-sequence stars derived from Gaia EDR3 and 2MASS from McBride et al. (2021), their Table 5.

For complete details, see the original paper: https://iopscience.iop.org/article/10.3847/1538-3881/ac2432


Columns
-------

source_id - Unique identifier in the Gaia (e)DR3 source catalogue
l - Galactic longitude [deg]
b - Galactic latitude [deg]
age - Age [log yrs]
age_std - Error on age [log yrs]
pms - Pre-main-sequence probability []
pms_std - Error on pre-main-sequence probability []
av - Predicted extinction along the line of sight (positional only) [mag]
av_std - Uncertainty in the predicted extinction [mag]
