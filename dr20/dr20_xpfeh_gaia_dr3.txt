Summary
-------

Robust data-driven metallicities for 120 million stars from Gaia XP spectra. See Andrae et al. (2023).


Columns
-------

source_id - Gaia EDR3 source_id
in_training_sample - Boolean flag indicating if the star is in the training sample
mh_xgboost - XGBoost estimate of [M/H].
teff_xgboost - XGBoost estimate of T_eff [K]
logg_xgboost - XGBoost estimate of log g [log(cm s-2)]
