Summary
-------

Geometric and photogeometric distances to 1.47 billion stars in Gaia Early Data Release 3 from Bailer-Jones et al. (2021) (see https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2021AJ....161..147B/abstract).


Columns
-------

source_id - Source identifier, identical to the Gaia-EDR3 source_id
r_med_geo - Median of the geometric distance posterior [pc]
r_lo_geo - 16th percentile of the geometric distance posterior [pc]
r_hi_geo - 84th percentile of the geometric distance posterior
r_med_photogeo - Median of the photogeometric distance posterior [pc]
r_lo_photogeo - 16th percentile of the photogeometric distance posterior [pc]
r_hi_photogeo - 84th percentile of the photogeometric distance posterior [pc]
flag - Quality flag
