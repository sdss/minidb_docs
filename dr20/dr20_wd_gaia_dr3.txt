Summary
-------

Catalogue of white dwarfs in Gaia EDR3 Gentile Fusillo et al. (2021).


Columns
-------

wdjname - WD name (JHHMMSS.ss+DDMMSS.ss) equinox and epoch 2000
gaiaedr3 - Gaia EDR3 source_id

edr3name - GaiaEDR3 name (Gaia EDR3 NNNNNNNNNNNNNNNNNNN)
radeg - Gaia EDR3 Barycentric right ascension (ICRS) at Ep=2016.0 [deg]
e_radeg - Gaia EDR3 standard error on right ascension, e_RA=e_RA*cosDE [mas]

plx - Gaia EDR3 Absolute stellar parallax at Ep=2016.0 [mas]
e_plx - Gaia EDR3 parallax error [mas]
rplx - Gaia EDR3 Parallax divided by its standard error
zpcor - Zero point offset correction (Lindegren et al., 2020A&A...633A...2L)
pwd - The probability of being a white dwarf
density - The number of Gaia sources in the same ~= 50 arcsec sq. bin as this object
solid - Gaia EDR3 Solution ID
randomi - Gaia EDR3 Random index used to select subsets
epoch - Gaia EDR3 Reference epoch [yr]
pm - Gaia EDR3 Total proper motion [mas/yr]
e_pm - Gaia EDR3 Total proper motion error [mas/yr]
pmra - Gaia EDR3 Proper motion in RA, pmRA*cosDE [mas/yr]

pmde - Gaia EDR3 Proper motion in DE [mas/yr]
e_pmde - Gaia EDR3 Proper motion in DE error [mas/yr]
radecor - Correlation between right ascension and declination [-1/1]
raplxcor - Correlation between right ascension and parallax [-1/1]
rapmracor - Correlation between right ascension and proper motion in right ascension [-1/1]
rapmdecor - Correlation between right ascension and proper motion in declination [-1/1]
deplxcor - Correlation between declination and parallax [-1/1]
depmracor - Correlation between declination and proper motion in right ascension [-1/1]
depmdecor - Correlation between declination and proper motion in declination [-1/1]
plxpmracor - Correlation between parallax and proper motion in right ascension [-1/1]
plxpmdecor - Correlation between parallax and proper motion in declination [-1/1]
pmrapmdecor - Correlation between proper motion in right ascension and proper motion in declination [-1/1]
nal - Total number of observations AL
nac - Total number of observations AC
ngal - Number of good observations AL

gofal - Goodness of fit statistic of model wrt along-scan observations
chi2al - AL chi-square value
epsi - Excess noise of the source [mas]
sepsi - Significance of excess noise
solved - Which parameters have been solved for?
apf - Primary or secondary
nueff - Effective wavenumber of the source used in the astrometric solution [um-1]
pscol - Astrometrically estimated pseudocolour of the source [um]
e_pscol - Standard error of the pseudocolour of the source [um]
rapscolcorr - Correlation between right ascension and pseudocolour [-1/1]
depscolcorr - Correlation between declination and pseudocolour [-1/1]
plxpscolcorr - Correlation between parallax and pseudocolour [-1/1]
pmrapscolcorr - Correlation between proper motion in right ascension and pseudocolour [-1/1]
pmdepscolcorr - Correlation between proper motion in declination and pseudocolour [-1/1]
matchobsa - Matched FOV transits used in the AGIS solution
nper - Number of visibility periods used in Astrometric solution
amax - The longest semi-major axis of the 5--10 error ellipsoid [mas]
matchobs - The total number of field-of-view transits matched to this source
newmatchobs - The number of transits newly incorporated into an existing source in the current cycle
matchobsrm - The number of transits removed from an existing source in the current cycle
ipdgofha - Amplitude of the IPD GoF versus position angle of scan
ipdgofhp - Phase of the IPD GoF versus position angle of scan
ipdfmp - Percent of successful-IPD windows with more than one peak
ipdfow - Percent of transits with truncated windows or multiple gate
ruwe - Renormalised unit weight error
sdsk1 - Degree of concentration of scan directions across the source

sdsk3 - Degree of concentration of scan directions across the source
sdsk4 - Degree of concentration of scan directions across the source
sdmk1 - Mean position angle of scan directions across the source

sdmk3 - Mean position angle of scan directions across the source
sdmk4 - Mean position angle of scan directions across the source
dup - Source with multiple source identifiers
o_gmag - Number of observations (CCD transits) that contributed to the G mean flux and mean flux error
fg - Mean flux in the G-band [e-/s]
e_fg - Standard deviation of the G-band fluxes divided by sqrt(photGNObs) [e-/s]
rfg - Mean flux in the G-band divided by its error
gmag_vega - G-band mean magnitude (Vega) [mag]
e_gmag - Standard error of G-band mean magnitude (Vega) [mag]
fgcorr - Calibration corrected G-band mean flux [mag]
gmagcorr - Calibration corrected G magnitude [mag]
e_gmagcorr - Standard error of calibration corrected G magnitude [mag]
o_bpmag - Number of observations contributing to BP photometry
fbp - Mean flux in the integrated BP band [e-/s]
e_fbp - Error on the integrated BP mean flux [e-/s]
rfbp - Integrated BP mean flux divided by its error
bpmag - Integrated BP mean magnitude (Vega) [mag]
e_bpmag - Standard error of BP mean magnitude (Vega) [mag]
o_rpmag - Number of observations (CCD transits) that contributed to the integrated RP mean flux and mean flux error
frp - Mean flux in the integrated RP band [e-/s]
e_frp - Error on the integrated RP mean flux [e-/s]

rpmag - Integrated RP mean magnitude (Vega) [mag]

nbpcont - Number of BP contaminated transits
nbpblend - Number of BP blended transits
nrpcont - Number of RP contaminated transits
nrpblend - Number of RP blended transits
mode - Photometry processing mode

gmag_abs - Absolute G magnitude [mag]
bp_rp - BP-RP colour [mag]
bp_g - BP-G colour [mag]
g_rp - G-RP colour [mag]
rv - dr2_radial_velocity (not used)
e_rv - dr2_radial_velocity_error (not used)
o_rv - dr2_rv_nb_transits (not used)
teff - dr2_rv_template_teff (not used)
logg - dr2_rv_template_logg (not used)
fe_h - dr2_rv_template_fe_h (not used)
glon - Galactic longitude [deg]

elon - Ecliptic longitude [deg]
elat - Ecliptic latitude [deg]

chisqhe - chi square value of the fit (pure-He)
