Summary
-------

The Gaia DR3 catalogue of RR Lyrae stars. For more details, refer to https://gea.esac.esa.int/archive/documentation/GDR3/Gaia_archive/chap_datamodel/sec_dm_variability_tables/ssec_dm_vari_rrlyrae.html.


Columns
-------

solution_id - Solution Identifier
source_id - Unique source identifier
pf - Period corresponding to the fundamental pulsation mode in the G band time series [day]
pf_error - Uncertainty of the pf period [day]
p1_o - Period corresponding to the first overtone pulsation mode in the G band time series [day]
p1_o_error - Uncertainty of the p1_o period [day]
epoch_g - Epoch of the maximum of the light curve in the G band [Barycentric JD in TCB − 2 455 197.5 (day)]
epoch_g_error - Uncertainty on the epoch parameter epoch_g [day]
epoch_bp - Epoch of the maximum of the light curve in the BP band [Barycentric JD in TCB − 2 455 197.5 (day)]
epoch_bp_error - Uncertainty on the epoch parameter epoch_bp [day]
epoch_rp - Epoch of the maximum of the light curve in the RP band [Barycentric JD in TCB − 2 455 197.5 (day)]
epoch_rp_error - Uncertainty on the epoch parameter epoch_rp [day]
epoch_rv - Epoch of the minimum of the radial velocity curve [Barycentric JD in TCB − 2 455 197.5 (day)]
epoch_rv_error - Uncertainty on the epoch parameter epoch_rv [day]
int_average_g - Intensity-averaged magnitude in the G band [mag]
int_average_g_error - Uncertainty on int_average_g parameter [mag]
int_average_bp - Intensity-averaged magnitude in the BP band [mag]
int_average_bp_error - Uncertainty on int_average_bp parameter [mag]
int_average_rp - Intensity-averaged magnitude in the RP band [mag]
int_average_rp_error - Uncertainty on int_average_rp parameter [mag]
average_rv - Mean radial velocity [km s−1]
average_rv_error - Uncertainty on average_rv parameter [km s−1]
peak_to_peak_g - Peak-to-peak amplitude of the G band light curve [mag]
peak_to_peak_g_error - Uncertainty on the peak_to_peak_g parameter [mag]
peak_to_peak_bp - Peak-to-peak amplitude of the BP band light curve [mag]
peak_to_peak_bp_error - Uncertainty on the peak_to_peak_bp parameter [mag]
peak_to_peak_rp - Peak-to-peak amplitude of the RP band light curve [mag]
peak_to_peak_rp_error - Uncertainty on the peak_to_peak_rp parameter [mag]
peak_to_peak_rv - Peak-to-peak amplitude of the radial velocity curve [km s−1]
peak_to_peak_rv_error - Uncertainty on the peak_to_peak_rv parameter [km s−1]
metallicity - Metallicity of the star from the Fourier parameters of the light curve [dex]
metallicity_error - Uncertainty of the metallicity parameter [dex]
r21_g - Fourier decomposition parameter r21_g: A2/A1 (for G band)
r21_g_error - Uncertainty on the r21_g parameter: A2/A1 (for G band)
r31_g - Fourier decomposition parameter r31_g: A3/A1 (for G band)
r31_g_error - Uncertainty on the r31_g parameter: A3/A1 (for G band)
phi21_g - Fourier decomposition parameter phi21_g: phi2 - 2*phi1 (for G band) [rad]
phi21_g_error - Uncertainty on the phi21_g parameter: phi2 - 2*phi1 (for G band) [rad]
phi31_g - Fourier decomposition parameter phi31_g: phi3 - 3*phi1 (for G band) [rad]
phi31_g_error - Uncertainty on the phi31_g parameter: phi3 - 3*phi1 (for G band) [rad]
num_clean_epochs_g - Number of G FoV epochs used in the fitting algorithm
num_clean_epochs_bp - Number of BP epochs used in the fitting algorithm
num_clean_epochs_rp - Number of RP epochs used in the fitting algorithm
num_clean_epochs_rv - Number of radial velocity epochs used in the fitting algorithm
zp_mag_g - Zero point (mag) of the final model of the G band light curve [mag]
zp_mag_bp - Zero point (mag) of the final model of the BP band light curve [mag]
zp_mag_rp - Zero point (mag) of the final model of the RP band light curve [mag]
num_harmonics_for_p1_g - Number of harmonics used to model the first periodicity of the G-band light curve
num_harmonics_for_p1_bp - Number of harmonics used to model the first periodicity of the BP-band light curve
num_harmonics_for_p1_rp - Number of harmonics used to model the first periodicity of the RP-band light curve
num_harmonics_for_p1_rv - Number of harmonics used to model the first periodicity of the radial velocity curve
reference_time_g - Reference time of the Fourier modelled G-band light curve [Barycentric JD in TCB − 2 455 197.5 (day)]
reference_time_bp - Reference time of the Fourier modelled BP-band light curve [Barycentric JD in TCB − 2 455 197.5 (day)]
reference_time_rp - Reference time of the Fourier modelled RP-band light curve [Barycentric JD in TCB − 2 455 197.5 (day)]
reference_time_rv - Reference time of the Fourier modelled radial velocity curve [Barycentric JD in TCB − 2 455 197.5 (day)]
fund_freq1 - First frequency of the non-linear Fourier modelling [day-1]
fund_freq1_error - Error of the first frequency of the non-linear Fourier modelling [day-1]
fund_freq2 - Second frequency of the non-linear Fourier modelling in the G band [day-1]
fund_freq2_error - Error of the second frequency of the non-linear Fourier modelling in the G band [day-1]
fund_freq1_harmonic_ampl_g - Amplitudes of the Fourier model for the first frequency in the G band [mag]
fund_freq1_harmonic_ampl_g_error - Errors of the amplitudes of the Fourier model for the first frequency in the G band [mag]
fund_freq1_harmonic_phase_g - Phases of the Fourier model for the first frequency in the G band [rad]
fund_freq1_harmonic_phase_g_error - Errors of the phases of the Fourier model for the first frequency in the G band [rad]
fund_freq1_harmonic_ampl_bp - Amplitudes of the Fourier model for the first frequency in the BP band [mag]
fund_freq1_harmonic_ampl_bp_error - Errors of the amplitudes of the Fourier model for the first frequency in the BP band [mag]
fund_freq1_harmonic_phase_bp - Phases of the Fourier model for the first frequency in the BP band [rad]
fund_freq1_harmonic_phase_bp_error - Errors of the phases of the Fourier model for the first frequency in the BP band [rad]
fund_freq1_harmonic_ampl_rp - Amplitudes of the Fourier model for the first frequency in the RP band [mag]
fund_freq1_harmonic_ampl_rp_error - Errors of the amplitudes of the Fourier model for the first frequency in the RP band [mag]
fund_freq1_harmonic_phase_rp - Phases of the Fourier model for the first frequency in the RP band [rad]
fund_freq1_harmonic_phase_rp_error - Errors of the phases of the Fourier model for the first frequency in the RP band [rad]
fund_freq1_harmonic_ampl_rv - Amplitudes of the Fourier model for the first frequency of the radial velocity curve [km s−1]
fund_freq1_harmonic_ampl_rv_error - Errors of the amplitudes of the Fourier model for the first frequency of the radial velocity curve [km s−1]
fund_freq1_harmonic_phase_rv - Phases of the Fourier model for the first frequency of the radial velocity curve [rad]
fund_freq1_harmonic_phase_rv_error - Errors of the phases of the Fourier model for the first frequency of the radial velocity curve [rad]
best_classification - Best RR Lyrae classification estimate out of: 'RRc', 'RRab', 'RRd'
g_absorption - Interstellar absorption in the G-band [mag]
g_absorption_error - Error on the interstellar absorption in the G-band [mag]
