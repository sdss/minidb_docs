Summary
-------

Position cross-match of RAVE DR6 with Gaia EDR3. See https://www.rave-survey.org/metadata/ravedr6/dr6_x_gaiaedr3/.


Columns
-------

obsid - Unique Identifier for RAVE objects, Observation Date, Fieldname, Fibernumber
gaiae3 - Unique source identifier
id - Unique source designation
epoch - Reference epoch [yr]
radeg - Right ascension [deg]
e_radeg - Standard error of right ascension [mas]
dedeg - Declination [deg]
e_dedeg - Standard error of declination [mas]
plx - Parallax [mas]
e_plx - Standard error of parallax [mas]
pmra - Proper motion in right ascension direction [mas/yr]
e_pmra - Standard error of proper motion in right ascension direction [mas/yr]
pmde - Proper motion in declination direction [mas/yr]
e_pmde - Standard error of proper motion in declination direction [mas/yr]
dup - Source with duplicate sources
ng - Number of observations contributing to G photometry
fg - G-band mean flux [electron/s]
e_fg - Error on G-band mean flux [electron/s]
gmag - G-band mean magnitude [mag]
