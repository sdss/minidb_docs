Summary
-------

The table includes the field information, where a field is a unique pointing of the telescope on the sky.


Columns
-------

pk - The primary key. A sequential identifier.
racen - The Right Ascension of the center of the field.
deccen - The Declination of the center of the field.
version_pk - The primary key of the version in the dr19_version table.
cadence_pk - The primary key of the cadence in the dr19_cadence table.
observatory_pk - The primary key of the observatory in the dr19_observatory table.
position_angle - The position angle of the field E of N in degrees.
slots_exposures - Exposures assigned to each LST and sky brightness slot []
field_id - The idenifier of a field within a version of the survey plan.
