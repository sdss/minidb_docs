Summary
-------

GLIMPSE catalog (I, II and 3-D).

See full documentation at https://irsa.ipac.caltech.edu/data/SPITZER/GLIMPSE/doc/glimpse1_dataprod_v2.0.pdf

sn_8_0 - (flux)/(uncertainty in flux) in IRAC 8.0-band []
dens_3_6 - local source density in IRAC 3.6-band [#/sqarcmin]
