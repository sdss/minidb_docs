Summary
-------

White dwarf catalog of high-probablity WDs from Gentile Fusillo (2019) based on Gaia DR2.

See https://academic.oup.com/mnras/article/482/4/4570/5162857 for more information on the columns.



Columns
-------

wd - WD names from this catalog -- WD J + J2000 ra (hh mm ss.ss) + dec (dd mm ss.s), equinox and epoch 2000 []
dr2name - Unique Gaia source designation []
source_id - Unique Gaia DR2 source identifier []
source - Internal identifier (do not use) []
ra - Right ascension [deg]
e_ra - Standard error of right ascenscion [mas]
dec - Declination [deg]
e_dec - Standard error of declination [mas]
plx - Absolute stellar parallax of the source at J2015.5 [mas]
e_plx - Standard error of parallax [mas]
pmra - Proper motion in right ascension [mas/yr]
e_pmra - Standard error of proper motion in right ascension [mas/yr]
pmdec - Proper motion in right declination [mas/yr]
e_pmdec - Standard error of proper motion in declination [mas/yr]
epsi - Measure of the residuals in the astrometric solution [mas]
amax - 5-dimensional equivalent to the semimajor axis of the Gaia position error ellipse [mas]
fg_gaia - Gaia G-band mean flux [e-/s]
e_fg_gaia - Error on G-band mean flux [e-/s]
g_gaia_mag - Gaia G-band mean magnitude [mag]
fbp - Integrated G_BP mean flux [e-/s]
e_fbp - Error on integrated G_BP mean flux [e-/s]
bpmag - Integrated G_BP mean magnitude [mag]
frp - Integrated G_RP mean flux [e-/s]
e_frp - Error on integrated G_RP mean flux [e-/s]
rpmag - Integrated G_RP mean magnitude [mag]
e_br_rp - G_BP/G_RP execess factor
glon - Galactic longitude [deg]
glat - Galactic latitude [deg]
density - Number of Gaia sources around this object [sq.deg^-1]
ag - Extinction in the Gaia G band derived from E(B − V) values from Schlafly and  Finkbeiner [mag]
sdss - SDSS object name if available []
umag - SDSS u-band magnitude [mag]
e_umag - SDSS u-band magnitude uncertainty [mag]
gmag - SDSS g-band magnitude uncertainty [mag]
e_gmag - SDSS g-band magnitude [mag]
rmag - SDSS r-band magnitude uncertainty [mag]
e_rmag - SDSS r-band magnitude [mag]
imag - SDSS i-band magnitude uncertainty [mag]
e_imag - SDSS i-band magnitude [mag]
zmag - SDSS z-band magnitude uncertainty [mag]
e_zmag - SDSS z-band magnitude [mag]
pwd - Probability of being a white dwarf []
f_pwd - Flag on probability of being a white dwarf []
teffh - Effective temperature from fitting the dereddened G, GBP, and GRP absolute fluxes with pure-H model atmospheres [K]
e_teffh - Uncertainty on Teff from pure-H model atmospheres [K]
loggh - Surface gravity from fitting the dereddened G, GBP, and GRP absolute fluxes with pure-H model atmospheres [dex cgs]
e_loggh - Uncertainty on log g from pure-H model atmospheres [dex cgs]
massh - Stellar mass assuming pure-H model atmospheres [Solar masses]
e_massh - Uncertainty on the mass assuming pure-H model atmospheres [Solar masses]
chi2h - Chi^2 value of the pure-H fit []
teffhe - Effective temperature from fitting the dereddened G, GBP, and GRP absolute fluxes with pure-He model atmospheres [K]
e_teffhe - Uncertainty on Teff from pure-He model atmospheres [K]
logghe - Surface gravity from fitting the dereddened G, GBP, and GRP absolute fluxes with pure-He model atmospheres [dex cgs]
e_logghe - Uncertainty on log g from pure-He model atmospheres [dex cgs]
masshe - Stellar mass assuming pure-He model atmospheres [Solar masses]
e_masshe - Uncertainty on the mass assuming pure-H model atmospheres [Solar masses]
chisqhe - Chi^2 value of the pure-H fit []
