Summary
-------

The table contains the cross-match between the dr19_catalog targets and the dr19_legacy_survey_dr8 table.


Columns
-------

catalogid - The catalogid identifier in the dr19_catalog table.
target_id - The primary key identifier in the dr19_legacy_survey_dr8 table.
version_id - The internal version for the cross-match.
distance - The distance between the catalog and target coordinates if best=F.
best - Whether this is considered the best match between the catalog entry and dr19_legacy_survey_dr8.
