Summary
-------

This table contains basic information about a configuration of robots loaded at the telescope.


Columns
-------

configuration_id - Unique identifier
design_id - Unique identifier of the design loaded
comment - An optional comment
temperature - The ambient temperature when the design was loaded
epoch - The Julian Date (JD) when the design was loaded
calibration_version - The version of fps_calibration used, https://github.com/sdss/fps_calibrations/tags
