Summary
-------

This table stores the meta-data for the design, including its Design Mode and versioning information.


Columns
-------

design_id - The primary key. A sequential identifier.
design_mode_label - The primary key of the design_mode in the dr19_design_mode table.
mugatu_version - Software version of mugatu used to validate and ingest design.
run_on - Date that design was added to database.
assignment_hash - Hash of the assignments in the design.
design_version_pk - The primary key of the version in the dr19_version table.
