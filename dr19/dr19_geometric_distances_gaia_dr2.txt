Summary
-------

Bayesian distances from Gaia DR2 parameters from Bailer-Jones et al. 2018.

For complete details, see the original paper: https://iopscience.iop.org/article/10.3847/1538-3881/aacb21/pdf


Columns
-------

source_id - Unique Gaia DR2 source identifier []
r_est - Estimated distance [pc]
r_lo - Lower bound on the confidence interval of the estimated distance [pc]
r_hi - Upper bound on the confidence interval of the estimated distance [pc]
r_len - Length scale used in the prior for the distance estimation [pc]
result_flag - Result flag []
modality_flag - Number of modes in the posterior []
