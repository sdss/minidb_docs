Summary
-------

The table contains the cross-match between the dr18_catalog targets and the dr18_sdss_dr16_specobj table.


Columns
-------

catalogid - The catalogid identifier in the dr19_catalog table.
target_id - The primary key identifier in the dr19_sdss_dr16_specobj table.
version_id - The internal version for the cross-match.
distance - The distance between the catalog and target coordinates if best=F.
best - Whether this is considered the best match between the catalog entry and dr18_sdss_dr16_specobj.
