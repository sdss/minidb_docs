Summary
-------

The 2018 release of the XMM OM Serendipitous Ultraviolet Source Survey (XMM-SUSS4.1) Catalog.

For full details, please see https://heasarc.gsfc.nasa.gov/W3Browse/all/xmmomsuob.html and the original catalog paper (Page et al. 2012; https://academic.oup.com/mnras/article/426/2/903/976665)
