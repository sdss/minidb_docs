Summary
-------

The table contains signal-to-noise estimates for each 'camera', for each exposure. APOGEE is treated as one camera, while R1/2 and B1/2 are treated separately.


Columns
-------

pk - Unique identifier
exposure_pk - Unique identifier in the exposure table
camera_pk - Unique identifier in the camera table
sn2 - Signal-to-noise squared estimate from on-mountain quick reduction pipelines
comment - An optional comment
