Summary
-------

Catalog of pre-main-sequence stars derived from Gaia DR2 and 2MASS from McBride et al. (2021), their Table 4.

For complete details, see the original paper: https://iopscience.iop.org/article/10.3847/1538-3881/ac2432



Columns
-------

source_id - Gaia DR2 unique identifier []
ra - Right ascension [degrees]
dec - Declination [degrees]
av - Predicted extinction [mag]
yso - Pre-main-sequence probability []
yso_std - Error on pre-main-sequence probability []
age - Age [log yrs]
age_std - Error on age [log yrs]
