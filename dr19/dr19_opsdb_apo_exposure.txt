Summary
-------

The table contains exposures taken at the observatory.


Columns
-------

pk - Unique identifier []
configuration_id - Unique identifier in the configuration table []
exposure_no - An Exposure Number assigned by the on mountain pipelines []
comment - Optional comment []
start_time - The time an exposure started [datetime]
exposure_time - The length of the exposure [seconds]
exposure_flavor_pk - Unique identifier in the exposure_flavor table []
