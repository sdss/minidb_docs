Summary
-------

This catalogue contains photometric stellar parameters for 9+ million stars in common between the SkyMapper survey and Gaia DR2.

See https://skymapper.anu.edu.au/_data/sm-gaia/ for details.


Columns
-------

skymapper_object_id - SkyMapper object_id []
gaia_source_id - Gaia DR2 source_id []
teff - Effective temperature [K]
e_teff - Effective temperature uncertainty [K]
feh - Metallicity [dex]
e_feh - Metallicity uncertainty [dex]
