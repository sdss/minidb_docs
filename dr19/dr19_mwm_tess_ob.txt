Summary
-------

This table contains targets requested for the manual carton mwm_tess_ob.


Columns
-------

gaia_dr2_id - Gaia DR2 unique source identifier [] 
ra - Right Ascension (J2000) [degrees]
dec - Declination (J2000) [degrees]
h_mag - 2MASS H-band mangitude [mag]
instrument - Instrument requested for this target (only APOGEE for this carton) []
cadence - Cadence requested for this carton []
