Summary
-------

The holes in which the positioners sit for the FPS at each observatory.


Columns
-------

pk - The primary key. A sequential identifier.
row_num - The row of the hole in which the positioner is sitting.
column_num - The column of the hole in which the positioner is sitting.
holeid - The identifier for the hole in which the positioner is sitting.
observatory_pk - The primary key of the observatory in the dr19_observatory table.
