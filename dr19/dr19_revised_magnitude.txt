Summary
-------

This table stores magnitude information for a target. Optical magnitudes that are not selected from SDSS photometry have been converted to the SDSS system.

Entries in this revised_magnitude table only exist if there was an issue with the values in the original magnitude table. Therefore: if a carton_to_target_pk exists in this table, the values in this table should be used instead.

Columns
-------

revised_magnitude_pk - The primary key. A sequential identifier.
carton_to_target_pk - The primary key of the target in the dr19_carton_to_target table.
g - The optical g magnitude.
r - The optical r magnitude.
i - The optical i magnitude.
z - The optical z magnitude.
gaia_g - The Gaia G magnitude.
bp - The Gaia BP magnitude.
rp - The Gaia RP magnitude.
h - The IR H magnitude.
j -  The IR J magnitude.
k -  The IR K magnitude.
optical_prov - The source of the optical magnitudes.
