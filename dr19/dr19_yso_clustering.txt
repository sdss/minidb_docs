Summary
-------

YSO candidates from Kounkel et al. 2020.

Please see details in the original paper: https://iopscience.iop.org/article/10.3847/1538-3881/abc0e6


Columns
-------

source_id - Gaia DR2 source id []
twomass - 2MASS ID []
ra - Right ascention in J2000 reference frame [degrees]
dec - Declination in J2000 reference frame [degrees]
parallax - Parallax from Gaia DR2 [mas]
id - Identification of a parent group from Kounkel et al. (2020) []
g - Gaia (DR2) G band magnitude [mag]
bp - Gaia (DR2) BP band magnitude [mag]
rp - Gaia (DR2) RP band magnitude [mag]
j - 2MASS J band magnitude [mag]
h - 2MASS H band magnitude [mag]
k - 2MASS K band magnitude [mag]
age - Estimate of the age of the parent group from Kounkel et al. (2020) [log yr]
eage - Uncertainty in age [log yr]
av - Estimate of extinction of the parent group from Kounkel et al. (2020) [mag]
eav - Uncertainty in av [mag]
dist - Estimate of distance of the parent group from Kounkel et al. (2020) [pc]
edist - Uncertainty in dist [pc]
