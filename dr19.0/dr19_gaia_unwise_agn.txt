Summary
-------

AGN identified using information from WISE and Gaia DR2 from the catalog of Shu et al. 2019. See complete information on the columns in https://academic.oup.com/mnras/article/489/4/4741/5561523


Columns
-------

ra - Right ascension from Gaia DR2 [degrees]
dec - Declination from Gaia DR2 [degrees]
gaia_sourceid - Unique Gaia DR2 source identifier []
unwise_objid - Unique unWISE source identifier []
plx - Parallax from Gaia DR2 [mas]
plx_err - Error in parallax from Gaia DR2 [mas]
pmra - Proper motion in right ascension from Gaia DR2 [mas/yr]
pmra_err - Error in proper motion in right ascension from Gaia DR2 [mas/yr]
pmdec - Proper motion in declination from Gaia DR2 [mas/yr]
pmdec_err - Error in proper motinon in declination from Gaia DR2 [mas/yr]
plxsig - Parallax significance defined as parallax/parallax_error []
pmsig - Proper motion significance []
ebv - Galactic E(B-V) reddening from Schlegel et al. 1998 [mag]
n_obs - Number of observations contributing to Gaia DR2 G photometry []
g - Gaia DR2 G-band mean magnitude (extinction corrected) [mag]
bp - Gaia DR2 BP-band mean magnitude (extinction corrected) [mag]
rp - Gaia DR2 RP-band mean magnitude (extinction corrected) [mag]
w1 - unWISE W1-band magnitude [mag]
w2 - unWISE W2-band magnitude [mag]
bp_g - Gaia DR2 BP − G color (extinction corrected) [mag]
bp_rp - Gaia DR2 BP − RP color (extinction corrected) [mag]
g_rp - Gaia DR2 G − RP color (extinction corrected) [mag]
g_w1 - Gaia DR2 G − unWISE W1 color (extinction corrected) [mag]
gw_sep - Separation between a Gaia source and its unWISE counterpart [arcsec]
w1_w2 - unWISE W1 −W2 color [mag]
g_var - Variation in GaiaG-band flux []
bprp_ef - BP/RP excess factor from Gaia DR2 []
aen - Astrometric excess noise from Gaia DR2 [mas]
gof - Goodness-of-fit statistic of the astrometric solution from Gaia DR2 []
cnt1 - Number of Gaia DR2 sources within a 1 arcsec radius circular aperture []
cnt2 - Number of Gaia DR2 sources within a 2 arcsec radius circular aperture []
cnt4 - Number of Gaia DR2 sources within a 4 arcsec radius circular aperture []
cnt8 - Number of Gaia DR2 sources within a 8 arcsec radius circular aperture []
cnt16 - Number of Gaia DR2 sources within a 16 arcsec radius circular aperture []
cnt32 - Number of Gaia DR2 sources within a 32 arcsec radius circular aperture []
phot_z - Photometric redshift []
prob_rf - AGN probability []
