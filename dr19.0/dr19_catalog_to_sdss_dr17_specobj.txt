Summary
-------

The table contains the cross-match between the dr19_catalog targets and the dr19_sdss_dr17_specobj table.


Columns
-------

catalogid - The catalogid identifier in the dr19_catalog table.
target_id - The primary key identifier in the dr19_sdss_dr17_specobj table.
version_id - The internal version for the cross-match.
distance - The distance between the catalog and target coordinates if best=F.
best - Whether this is considered the best match between the catalog entry and dr19_sdss_dr17_specobj.
plan_id - Identifier of the cross-matching plan used to generate this file.
added_by_phase - The phase of the cross-match in which this target was added.
